Upstream Web Design

Upstream is a conservation company focused on the conservation of marine species and our oceans. They needed a complete web design that showcases what they stand for and ways to help. Upstream wanted a website that was friendly but also portrayed the seriousness of the topic. The priority was highlighting key elements and giving a lot of information as well as giving clear ways that people can help.

For this project I completely designed all pages of the website in an efficient way to be passed onto a web developer for hosting.
