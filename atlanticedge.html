Atlantic Edge Branding Package

Atlantic Edge is a real estate group focussing on waterfront homes. What began in Atlantic Canada has spread country-wide. This is a conceptualization of corporate branding and design.

For this project, I was the lead designer creating the logo and all corporate branding elements. There was a focus on keeping it luxurious and inviting and keeping the same brand voice across all areas.
