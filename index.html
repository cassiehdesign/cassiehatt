I'm Cassie
Graphic designer and photographer with experience in branding, advertising, print, and web design. Based in Calgary, Alberta.

A Little About Me

With an advanced diploma and over 4 years experience as a full-time graphic designer, I'm passionate about combining my skills in photography and art to make my designs memorable and stand out. I focus on the problem and pride myself in figuring out how my design can be the solution.

I have worked on many different projects across all platforms, collaborating with multiple departments and teams to output the highest quality work that drives results while meeting the client's needs. I thrive off of learning new things and hold onto any opportunity that helps me further my design skills. I am passionate and hard-working, and never stop creating.

When I'm not designing I enjoy playing boardgames (Azul, Wingspan, and Clank are great!), hiking, paddleboarding, painting, crocheting, and relaxing on the couch with my two kitties!
